Item Status


Item - Can be done, soon, due, or overdue

Subsections are dependent on items, and rooms on Subsections

If something was marked complete today or done within 75% of the window, it's done
Marked as low priority or 75%-85% it's soon, 85%-100% it's due, and >100% it's overdue

Statuses have weights

Done - 1
Soon - .9
Due - .5
Overdue - .4

If the weighted average is greater than .9 it's done, .75 it's soon, .6 is due, and anything else is overdue
